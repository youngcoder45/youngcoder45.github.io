## Code of Conduct

This project follows a basic standard of respectful and professional collaboration.

## Expectations

* Be respectful and considerate in communication
* Accept constructive feedback
* Focus on improving the project
* Maintain a positive and inclusive environment

## Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment or offensive language
* Personal attacks
* Spam or irrelevant contributions
* Disruptive or abusive behavior

## Contributions

All contributors are expected to:

* Follow project guidelines
* Keep discussions relevant and productive
* Respect maintainers' decisions

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right to:

* Remove inappropriate content
* Reject contributions that do not follow guidelines
* Restrict participation if necessary

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior:

* Contact via GitHub profile or email
* Provide clear details of the issue

## Note

This is a personal project. The goal is to maintain a clean, focused, and respectful development environment.
